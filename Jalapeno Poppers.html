Jalapeno Poppers

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 oz) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • ¼ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 (1 oz) package dry ranch salad dressing mix
  • 1 ½ tsp garlic powder
  • 20 large jalapeno peppers, halved and seeded
  • 1 lb sliced bacon, cut in half

Instructions

  1. Preheat an oven to 200°C
  2. Stir together cream cheese, cheddar, mayonnaise, ranch dressing mix, and garlic powder until evenly blended
  3. Spoon some of the mixture into each jalapeno half and wrap with half a bacon strip
  4. Secure with a toothpick
  5. Arrange wrapped jalapeno halves onto a broiler pan
  6. Bake until bacon begins to brown (~20 minutes)
