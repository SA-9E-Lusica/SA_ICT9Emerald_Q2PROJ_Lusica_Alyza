Lemon Cheescake

Ingredients

For Base

  • 200 g Ginger nut biscuits, crushed
  • 80 g Unsalted butter, melted

For Filling

  • 300 ml Double cream
  • 400 g Full fat cream cheese
  • 100 g Icing sugar, sifted
  • 2 Lemons, zest
  • 85 ml Lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Combine crushed biscuits with melted butter and press into a tin
  2. Chill in the fridge while you make the topping
  3. Pour double cream into bowl, then add cream cheese, icing sugar, and zest of 2 lemons
  4. Add the Lemon juice (DON'T use more than 85 ml s it may become too runny)
  5. Whisk until it's very thick â€“ like ice cream. Use an electric whisk if you have one
  6. Remove base from fridge and add cheesecake mixture, smoothing the top
  7. Pop into the fridge for at least 4 hours
  8. Remove from tin then sprinkle edges with remaining lemon zest before serving
