No Bake Pumpkin Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 (0.25 oz) package unflavored gelatin
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp ground ginger
  • ½ tsp ground nutmeg
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 (14 oz) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 (15 oz) can pumpkin puree
  • 1 (9 in) prepared graham cracker crust

Instructions

  1. Combine gelatin, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, and salt in a heavy saucepan
  2. Stir in condensed milk and beaten eggs, mixing well
  3. Let stand one minute, then place on burner over low heat, stirring constantly until gelatin dissolves and mixture thickens (~10 minutes)
  4. Remove from heat
  5. Stir in pumpkin until thoroughly combined
  6. Pour mixture into graham cracker crust
  7. Chill for at least 3 hours before serving
