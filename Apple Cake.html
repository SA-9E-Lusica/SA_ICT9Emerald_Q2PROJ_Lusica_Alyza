Apple Cinnamon Cake

Ingredients

  • 150 g Butter, room temp
  • 150 g Caster sugar
  • 2 Eggs, Medium
  • 200 g Self-raising flour
  • 2 tsp Ground cinnamon
  • 300g Apples (about 4 pcs), chopped evenly
  • 25 g Flaked almonds

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 180Â°C
  2. Beat butter and sugar until light and fluffy in a large bowl
  3. Add eggs, flour, and cinnamon
  4. Beat until well combined and there are no flour pockets
  5. Fold in apples by hand
  6. Line 20cm x 20cm square tin with baking paper or parchment
  7. Pour in mixture and smooth gently
  8. Sprinkle over flaked almonds
  9. Bake for 30-35 minutes
