Cheese & Asparagus Puff Pastries

Ingredients

  • 300 g Asparagus, tough ends removed
  • 150 g Garlic and herb cream cheese
  • 150 g Cheddar cheese, grated
  • 320 g Puff pastry sheet
  • 1 Egg, beaten (or 1 tbsp milk)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C
  2. Boil a large saucepan of water with plenty of salt
  3. Add the asparagus for 2 minutes
  4. Drain and rinse in cold water
  5. Lay the puff pastry out and cut into 6 squares
  6. Put onto a baking sheet lined with baking paper
  7. Add a dollop of the cream cheese to the middle of each one
  8. Lay asparagus on top, diagonally across the squares, and sprinkle the cheddar on top
  9. Fold in the uncovered, diagonal corners, and press to join them on top of the asparagus
  10. Brush with the egg (or milk)
  11. Bake for 15-20 minutes until crisp and golden
