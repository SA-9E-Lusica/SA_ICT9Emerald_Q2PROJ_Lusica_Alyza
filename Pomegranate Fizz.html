Pomegranate Fizz

Ingredients

  • 5 fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tbsps agave syrup (or honey)
  • 1 fl oz fresh lime juice
  • 3 fl oz pomegranate juice
  • 4 fl oz club soda
  • 1 lime wedge
  • ice cubes (as needed)

Instructions

  1. In a shaker, add basil leaves with lime juice, agave syrup, and mash with a cocktail muddler
  2. Add pomegranate juice to the shaker and shake well
  3. Fill a glass with ice cubes and strain shaker contents into the glass
  4. Top with club soda and garnish with lime wedge
