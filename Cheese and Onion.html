Cheese & Onion Pasties

Ingredients

  • 400 g Potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 30 g Salted butter
  • 2 Large onions, peeled and finely chopped
  • 125 g Strong cheddar, grated
  • 375 g Puff pastry
  • Salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil potatoes in large pan of salted water for 15-20 minutes until soft
  2. While boiling, melt butter in small frying pan and gently fry onions for 5-10 minutes until soft and transparent (try to not brown them too much)
  3. Preheat oven to 200°C
  4. When potatoes are cooked, drain them and add onions (and any butter left in bottom of the pan), along with cheese and plenty of salt and pepper
  5. Mash well and allow to cool
  6. Roll out puff pastry and cut into 8 rectangles
  7. Share potato mixture across 4 of them
  8. Brush around the edges with beaten egg and sit the other 4 ‘lids’ on top
  9. Seal edges using a fork and brush the top with the remaining egg
  10. Place on baking sheet lined with baking paper/parchment
  11. Bake for 15-20 minutes until very golden
