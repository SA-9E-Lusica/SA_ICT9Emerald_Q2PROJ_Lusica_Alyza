Courgette Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 Onion, Chopped
  • 3 Garlic cloves, Crushed
  • 1 kg Courgette , Chopped
  • 1 Leek, Chopped
  • 1.8 ltrs Vegetable stock
  • 125 g Frozen peas/petit pois
  • 75 g Parmesan

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan
  2. Fry onions and garlic until soft
  3. Add courgettes and leeks and cook for another 5 minutes
  4. Add in your stock and bring to the boil
  5. Boil for around 15-20 minutes until courgettes are soft
  6. Stir in parmesan
  7. Blend to give a smooth consistency
  8. Optional: Serve with either a dollop of crème fraîche, some croutons, or some chives sprinkled on top
