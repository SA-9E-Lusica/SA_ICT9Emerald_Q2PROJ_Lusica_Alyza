Blackberry Margarita

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp coarse salt
  • 1 lime wedge
  • 2 cups ice (or as needed)
  • 12 blackberries
  • 2 fl oz silver tequila
  • 1 fl oz triple sec
  • 1 fl oz simple syrup
  • 3/4 fl oz lime juice

Instructions

  1. Sprinkle salt onto a plate
  2. Moisten rim of a glass with lime wedge
  3. Press moistened rim into salt
  4. Fill glass with ice
  5. Fill cocktail shaker with 10 blackberries (reserve 2 blackberries for garnish)
  6. Use muddler to crush berries to release their juices
  7. Add tequila, triple sec, simple syrup, and lime juice to shaker and add 1 cup of ice
  8. Seal and shake vigorously until outside is frosted (10-15 seconds)
