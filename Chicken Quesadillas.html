Chicken Queasadillas

Ingredients

  • 300 g Cooked chicken (approx. 2-3 breasts)
  • 400 g Black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 340 g Sweetcorn, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 Red onion, peeled and finely chopped
  • 2 tsp Fajita seasoning
  • 2 tsp Sunflower oil
  • 8 large flour tortillas
  • 200 g grated Mozzarella

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix the chicken, beans, sweetcorn, onion and fajita seasoning
  2. Preheat 1/2 tsp of the oil in a large frying pan over a medium heat
  3. Sit one tortilla in the pan, sprinkle over a small amount of the cheese
  4. Spoon on a quarter of the chicken and bean mixture (work reasonably quickly so the bottom doesnâ€™t start to burn)
  5. Sprinkle some more cheese on top and sit another tortilla on top (use bottom of a clean saucepan to weigh down quesadilla)
  6. Cook both sides for 3 minutes
  7. Repeat this process to cook all the quesadillas
