Granola Bars

Ingredients

  • 100 g Unsalted butter
  • 3 tbsp Runny honey
  • 100 g Light brown sugar
  • 200 g Porridge oats
  • 200 g Mixed seeds
  • 100 g Dried fruit
  • 1 tsp Ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 160°C
  2. Line small shallow tin (~20cm x 20cm) with baking paper
  3. Warm the butter, honey, and sugar gently in a saucepan (DON'T let the mixture boil)
  4. Add oats, seeds, dried fruit, and cinnamon to the saucepan
  5. Mix until everything is nicely coated
  6. Pour into tin then press down lightly
  7. Bake for 35–40 minutes until golden brown all over
  8. Allow to cool completely in the tin before you slice
