Thin Mint Muddy Buddies

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cups thin mint cookies, finely crushed
  • 2 tbsp creme de menthe candy (e.g. Andes® mints), chopped
  • 1 1/2 cups chocolate chips, semisweet
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 tsp mint extract
  • 6 cups crispy cereal squares (e.g. Chex cereal)
  • 3/4 cups confectioners sugar

Instructions

  1. Add semisweet chocolate chips, butter, and mint extract to a microwave-safe bowl
  2. Microwave for 1 minute and stir
  3. Continue to microwave at 15 second intervals, stirring well each time
  4. Continue until mixture is smooth
  5. Add cereal to a large bowl
  6. Pour melted mint chocolate over cereal and stir to coat thoroughly
  7. Add coated cereal to large resealable bag. Pour in confectioner’s sugar and crushed thin mints
  8. Shake it all together until cereal is evenly coated in the mixture
  9. Pour cereal mixture out onto baking pan
  10. Sprinkle chopped mints over the top
  11. Once mixture is cooled and set, transfer to airtight container
