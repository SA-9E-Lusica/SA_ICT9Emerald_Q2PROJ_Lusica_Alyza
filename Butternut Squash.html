Butternut Squash

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 Onions, peeled and chopped
  • 4 cloves Garlic, peeled and crushed
  • 1 tsp ground Cumin
  • 1 tsp dried Oregano
  • 1 tsp mild Chilli powder
  • 1 kg Butternut squash cubes, fresh or frozen
  • 700 ml hot Vegetable stock
  • 1 tsp Sea salt
  • Ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat
  2. Add onion and garlic and cook for 5–10 minutes, stirring often, until onions start to sotfen
  3. Reduce heat to low and add cumin, oregano, chilli powder and cook for another 3–5 minutes, stirring, until spices are just fragrant
  4. Add butternut squash, stock, and plenty of salt and pepper
  5. Put lid on and turn heat to medium/low and cook for 20–25 minutes (or until squash is very soft)
  6. Use either a blender or a hand blender to puree until smooth
