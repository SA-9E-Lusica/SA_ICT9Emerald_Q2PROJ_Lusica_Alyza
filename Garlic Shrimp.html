Garlic Shrimp Pasta Bake

Ingredients

  • 1 (10 oz) package penne pasta
  • 3 tbsp butter, divided
  • 1 tsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 lb uncooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 tbsp chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 2 tsp chopped fresh dill
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ½ cup grated Romano cheese
  • ¼ cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 230°C
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil
  3. Add penne and cook, stirring occasionally, until al dente (~11 minutes)
  4. Drain and set aside until needed
  5. While the pasta is cooking, heat 1 tbsp butter and oil in a large oven-safe skillet over medium heat
  6. Add garlic and cook until fragrant (~30 seconds)
  7. Add shrimp, 2 tbsp parsley, and dill
  8. Cook until shrimp are bright pink on the outside and the meat is opaque (1 1/2-2 minutes per side)
  9. Transfer shrimp to a plate, leaving any cooking juices in the pan
  10. Melt remaining 2 tbsp butter in the skillet over medium heat
  11. Whisk in flour until fully combined and starting to turn golden (30 seconds-1 minute)
  12. Whisk in chicken broth until combined, then repeat with milk
  13. Stir in tomatoes, lemon juice, salt, and pepper; bring to a simmer
  14. Add 1/2 cup Parmesan and Romano; stir until cheeses begin to melt and sauce is creamy
  15. Add 1 tbsp mozzarella cheese, then mix in 1/2 of the cooked pasta
  16. Add more pasta if there is enough sauce to coat it
  17. Stir in shrimp, then top with remaining mozzarella, parmesan, and parsley
  18. Bake until golden on top and bubbling around the edges (~8 to 10 minutes)
