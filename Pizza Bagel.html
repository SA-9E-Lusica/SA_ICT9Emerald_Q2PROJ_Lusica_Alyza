Mini Pizza Bagels

Ingredients

  • 8 mini bagels, split
  • ¼ cup pizza sauce
  • ⅓ cup pizza cheese blend, shredded
  • 16 slices turkey pepperoni (e.g. Hormel)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C
  2. Line baking sheet with aluminum foil
  3. Arrange bagels, cut-side up, on the baking sheet
  4. Spoon thin layer sauce over each bagel half and sprinkle with cheese
  5. Place two pepperoni slices onto each bagel half
  6. Bake until cheese is melted and pepperoni is lightly browned (~6 minutes)
