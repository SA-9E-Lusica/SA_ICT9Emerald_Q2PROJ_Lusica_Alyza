Easy Shake and Bake Chicken

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs chicken, cut into pieces
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • ¼ tsp sage
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ cup butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 230°C
  2. Mix flour, salt, paprika, sage, and pepper together in a resealable plastic bag
  3. Place chicken parts into the bag; seal and shake until well coated
  4. Melt butter in a 9x13-in baking dish in the preheated oven
  5. Arrange coated chicken pieces in the baking dish skin-side down and bake for 20 minutes
  6. Turn chicken pieces over and bake until no longer pink at the bone and the juices run clear (~20 minutes or more)
  7. An instant-read thermometer inserted near the bone should read 74°C
