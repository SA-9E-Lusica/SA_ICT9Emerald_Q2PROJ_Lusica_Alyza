Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 75 g Unsalted Butter, melted
  • 100 g Caster sugar
  • 2 Eggs, Medium
  • 225 g Self-raising flour
  • 3 large bananas (very ripe), peeled and mashed

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C
  2. Grease tin with soft butter (or line with baking paper)
  3. Break eggs into mixing bowl
  4. Add melted butter, sugar, flour, and mashed bananas
  5. Mix very well (either by hand or an electric mixer)
  6. Spoon into tin, smoothing out top
  7. Bake for 40–50 mins at 180°C until top is golden and a knife comes out clean
  8. Allow to cool in the tin for 10–15 minutes and then turn out carefully
