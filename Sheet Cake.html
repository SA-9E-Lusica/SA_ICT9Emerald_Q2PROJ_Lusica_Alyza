Texas Sheet Cake

Ingredients

For Cake

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup water
  • 5 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder

For Icing

  • 6 tbsp milk
  • 5 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ cup butter
  • 4 cups confectioners' sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup walnuts, chopped (Optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C
  2. Grease and flour a 10x15-inch jelly roll pan
  3. Combine flour, sugar, baking soda, and salt in a mixing bowl
  4. Beat in sour cream and eggs
  5. Melt butter in a saucepan over low heat
  6. Stir in water and cocoa powder
  7. Bring mixture to a boil then remove from the heat
  8. Allow to cool slightly, then stir cocoa mixture into the flour and egg mixture until blended
  9. Pour batter into the prepared pan
  10. Bake for around 20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean
  11. While baking, combine milk, cocoa powder, and butter in a large saucepan over medium heat for the icing
  12. Bring to a boil, then remove from heat
  13. Stir in confectioners' sugar and vanilla, then fold in walnuts, mixing until blended
  14. Spread icing over warm cake
