Kale Chips

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch kale
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp flaked sea salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 150Â°C
  2. Line rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper
  3. Carefully remove kale leaves from the thick stems
  4. Tear into bite-sized pieces
  5. Wash and thoroughly dry kale
  6. Drizzle kale with oil and toss to combine
  7. Spread out in an even layer on baking sheet without overlapping
  8. Bake until edges start to brown (NOT burnt; ~20-30 minutes)
