Pork Tenderloin Diablo

Ingredients

  • 1 (1 lb) whole pork tenderloin
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tsp vegetable oil
  • ½ cup chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp extra-hot prepared horseradish
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • ¼ tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tbsp cold unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp chopped fresh chives

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C
  2. Season pork with salt and black pepper
  3. Heat oil in an ovenproof skillet over high heat
  4. Add pork; cook until browned on one side (~3-4 minutes)
  5. Flip pork; transfer skillet to oven
  6. Cook until pork browned and still slightly pink in center (20-25 minutes)
  7. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 63°C
  8. Transfer pork to a plate
  9. Discard any excess oil from skillet
  10. Place skillet over medium-high heat
  11. Add chicken broth; bring to a boil, scraping any browned bits off bottom of skillet
  12. Whisk in cream, horseradish, Dijon mustard, and cayenne pepper
  13. Continue cooking until reduced to a thick sauce (3-4 minutes)
  14. Off heat, whisk in cold butter, then stir in chives
  15. Slice pork into 1/2 in slices; top with sauce
