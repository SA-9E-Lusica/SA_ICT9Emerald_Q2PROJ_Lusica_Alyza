Spiced Thai Iced Coffee

Ingredients

  • 5 cups water
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • 5 pods star anise
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4 pods cardamom, crushed
  • 8 red rooibos tea bags
  • ¾ cup sweetened condensed milk
  • Ice

Instructions

  1. Place water, sugar, star anise, cinnamon stick, and cardamom in a saucepan and bring to a boil
  2. Remove from heat and add tea bags
  3. Let tea steep and cool to room temperature (~1 hour)
  4. Remove tea bags and strain out whole spices then discard
  5. Whisk in condensed milk
  6. Pour into a large pitcher and refrigerate until flavors combine (at least 1 hour)
  7. Serve tea over ice
